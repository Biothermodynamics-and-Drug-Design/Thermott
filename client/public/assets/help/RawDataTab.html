• Upload your raw fluorescence data by clicking |Raw Data Upload| button
  • In the the dialog select:
    • Your file to be uploaded
    • The file's format
    • Select melting curve model used for fitting
    • Click |Upload|
  • After upload at list of your experiment's well will appear on the left
  • Clicking on an experiment well will show it's melting curve, fitted model parameters on right of the screen
  • You can select multiple experiment wells simultaneusly by click with the Ctrl key
  • You can refit the data: left-click and drag on the graph to select a new fitting interval
  • You can view a summary of your fitted experiment wells and their respective Tm values by clicking |Summary| button in the lower left
  • A dialog will appear showing a table of your data
  • You can export the data by clicking |Export| button and choosing whether to download a CSV file or to copy to clipboard. Clipboard can be then pasted in your favourite spreadsheet software
  • You can also view a heatmap of Tm values by click |Heatmap| button in the lower left
  • The image can be downloaded in SVG format
